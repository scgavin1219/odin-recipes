Quese-Dylan Recipe!

Description

I was trying to get a little fancy with my plum pie recipe, but that's not really who I am in the kitchen. I am not about taste, flavor, or aesthetics. Food should be prepared and devoured efficiently and the quese-dylan delivers.

The best part about this recipe is that you can literally add any ingredients you want. Add some veggies, add some tomato sauce and pepperonis and make a mexican pizza. The kitchen is yours and do whatever you want in it. Let's get cooking baby!

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • pepperjack cheese
  • LITERALLY WHATEVER THE FUCK YOU WANT

Steps

  1. Pre-heat a cast-iron (don't use some bullshit non-stick pan, it doesn't heat the quesedilla evenly!) to medium heat.
  2. Put one tortilla in the pan and sprinkle pepperjack (Don't use any other cheese. It's pepperjack or nothing. All other chesses are inferior) gratuitously. Add any other ingredients you would like and put a second tortilla on top.
  3. Wait approximately 3 to 4 minutes or until the cheese is apprximately half melted and flip the quesedilla.
  4. Cook for another 3 minutes
  5. Quarter the quesedilla and eat!
